# Midnight Snacks

*midnight*
Sometimes you just need a little something.

* Lentils — a small bowl hits the spot. Try [Plain Lentils](#plain-lentils) (see
  page \pageref{plain-lentils}) or [Brown Lentil Dal](#brown-lentil-dal) (see page
  \pageref{brown-lentil-dal}).
* Quesadillas (see page \pageref{sweet-potato-black-bean-quesadilla}) — quick and
  satisfying.
* Sushi (see page \pageref{sushi-rice}) — if you have leftover sushi rice, it's
  ready in minutes.
* Boiled Eggs (see page \pageref{rice-cooker-boiled-eggs}) — a perfect little
  snack.
