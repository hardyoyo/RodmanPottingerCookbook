# Liam's Crispy Breakfast Potatoes

*breakfast, vegan, gluten-free*

**2-4 Servings**

---

- *4-5 medium* russet potatoes
- *1-2 T* avocado oil
- *1 t* garlic powder
- *1 t* onion powder
- *1/2 t* paprika powder
- *1/2 t* smoked paprika powder
- *1/2 t* dried thyme
- *1 t* dried oregano
- *1 t* sea salt
- *1/2 t* freshly ground black pepper
- *1/8-1/4 t* chipotle powder (or cayenne, optional)
- flaky sea salt (for serving, optional)

---

Preheat your oven to 450F (230C). Wash and peel the potatoes (you can
leave the skin on if you prefer). Dice them into evenly sized pieces
and add to a large baking sheet lined with parchment paper. The more
the potatoes are spread out on the baking sheet, the crispier they’ll
get. Use a paper towel to absorb as much moisture out of the potatoes.

Drizzle with avocado oil and toss. Mix all the spices in a separate
bowl and sprinkle evenly over the potatoes. Toss to coat well.

Roast in the preheated oven for 15 minutes. Gently toss the potatoes
to flip, turn on the broiler, and roast for another 10 minutes. If you
don’t have a broiler function, just keep the oven at 450F. Keep an eye
on the potatoes as broilers get very hot and could burn them quickly.

Serve with scrambled eggs, veggies, other savory breakfast dishes, or
on their own with ketchup. Sprinkle with flaky sea salt for serving if
desired.

Store leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge for 3-4 days.
Reheat in a 350F (175C) oven for 10-15 minutes or until hot.
