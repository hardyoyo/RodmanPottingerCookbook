# Tacos Al Vapor

*tacos, potato, mexican, vegan, freezer-friendly, air fryer*

**4 Servings**

---

- *6-8 medium* potatoes, cubed
- *1* yellow onion, finely diced
- *1* jalapeño, finely diced
- *1/2 (4 oz) can* green chiles
- salt, to taste
- cumin, to taste
- chili powder, to taste
- mushroom powder (or bullion), to taste
- *1-2 T* soy milk (as needed)
- *15* fresh corn tortillas
- oil spray

---

Cube potatoes and steam in an Instant Pot for 5 minutes. Meanwhile,
dice the onion finely; put half in the freezer for later and sauté the
other half in a skillet until just turning brown. Add diced jalapeño,
green chiles, and spices (salt, cumin, chili powder, mushroom powder —
whatever suits you) and cook briefly.

Transfer the cooked potatoes to a mixing bowl. Add the sautéed onion
mixture and mash together — keep it chunky, not smooth. Splash in soy
milk if the filling seems too dry.

Steam 15 corn tortillas in a rice cooker until pliable but sticky.
Spoon about 1/4 C of filling onto half a tortilla, fold it over like
an empanada, and press to seal — the sticky tortilla will hold itself
shut. Place the finished tacos on a wax-paper-lined cookie sheet. When
the sheet is full, spray the tacos with oil and freeze for a few
hours. Once frozen solid, transfer to a zip-top bag.

To serve, pop 3-4 tacos in an air fryer at 350°F for 12 minutes. If
still cold in the middle, cook 2-3 minutes more.
