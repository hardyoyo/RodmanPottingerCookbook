# Marinated Tofu

*tofu, staple, vegan*

**4 Servings**

---

- *1 package* extra-firm tofu, drained and pressed
- *1/2 C* Mirin
- *3 T* Tamari
- *2 T* rice wine vinegar
- *1 T* sesame oil
- *2 t* Chile Garlic Sauce (page \pageref{chile-garlic-sauce})
- *1 t* minced ginger
- *2 t* minced garlic

---

Press the tofu in a tofu press to remove excess moisture. Whisk
together the mirin, tamari, rice wine vinegar, sesame oil, Chile
Garlic Sauce, ginger, and garlic for the marinade. Slice the tofu into
slabs, place in a covered container, pour the marinade over, and
refrigerate for 4 to 24 hours.

Grill over medium heat, about 7 minutes per side. Or bake in a
preheated 400°F oven for about 25 minutes, flipping halfway through,
until golden and firm.
