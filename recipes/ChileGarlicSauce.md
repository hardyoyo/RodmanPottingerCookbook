# Chile Garlic Sauce

*staple, sauce, vegan*

**3/4 Cup**

---

- *1/2 C* sambal oelek
- *3 t* minced garlic
- *2 T* olive oil
- *1 T* agave syrup

---

Dump everything into a small food processor and blend until smooth. Pour into a
squeeze bottle. Keeps in the fridge for months.
