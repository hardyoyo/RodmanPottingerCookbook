# Chilaquiles
A great way to use up corn tortillas.

*breakfast*

**5 Servings**

---

- *12* corn tortillas (or more)
- *Half* 8oz. can of green chillies
- *1 t* garlic, minced
- *1 T* olive oil
- *1* large onion
- *8* eggs, scrambled
- *1/4 C* shredded cheese
