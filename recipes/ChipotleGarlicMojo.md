# Chipotle Garlic Mojo

*staple, sauce, vegan*

**1/3 Cup**

---

- *2 T* olive oil
- *1* chipotle pepper in adobo, minced
- *1 t* adobo sauce
- *2* garlic cloves, minced
- *1 T* lime juice
- *1 pinch* salt

---

Whisk everything together. Use immediately or refrigerate for up to a week.
